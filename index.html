ABOUT US

Welcome to our website!> We are a team with experience in the field of nanosatellites, GN&C systems, propulsion and subsystem development. Our journey began with the prestigious SUCHAI 2 & 3 and PlantSat Project of the Space and Planetary Exploration Laboratory (SPEL) of the University of Chile, and since then we have grown with the aspiration of becoming a recognized player in the space industry.

Our main goal is to provide innovative and affordable services and products through a lean development model.

Our mission is to revolutionize the aerospace industry through services and products with cutting-edge innovation and sustainable practices. We strive to make our solutions accessible to a wide range of customers.

Our vision is to be part of humanity's space exploration, and bring our clients closer to the limits of space.
